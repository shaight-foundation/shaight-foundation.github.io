Programatic rules-based financing

The Shaight Foundation is a charitable revolving fund that makes zero interest loans to people in need. We believe that the lack of liquidity in developing countries is one of the greatest challenges facing the modern world. In 2021, we set out to solve that problem. We use machine learning to identify applicants who are good credit risks and who present good opportunities to enrich their communities. From there, we deploy the needed funds in the most fiscally prudent way possible, allowing us to scale our impact exponentially over time.
