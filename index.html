Programatic rules-based financing

The Shaight Foundation is a charitable revolving fund that makes zero interest loans to people in need. We believe that the lack of liquidity in the developing world is one of the greatest challenges facing the modern world and we have set out to solve it. We use machine learning to identify applicants that are both good credit risks and good opportunities to lend a hand to a community. From there, we deploy the needed funds in the most fiscally prudent way possible, allowing us to scale our impact exponentially over time (see our white paper for more details).
