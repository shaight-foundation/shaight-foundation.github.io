Empowering Entrepreneurs in Developing Countries

Through programmatic rules-based microfinance loans, we've helped over 3100 entrepreneurs since 2021.


At the Sulit Foundation, we are proud to have supported over 3,100 small and family-owned businesses since 2021. These businesses are the heart of our communities, driving local economies, creating jobs, and fostering innovation. Through tailored resources, mentorship, and financial assistance, we've empowered these entrepreneurs to thrive despite the challenges they face.


Strengthening Communities

Small and family-owned businesses create vibrant neighborhoods and contribute to a diverse and dynamic economy. By supporting these businesses, we are not just helping them grow—we are enhancing the fabric of our communities, creating jobs, and ensuring that the unique voices of local entrepreneurs continue to be heard and valued.


A Proven Path to Success

Since 2021, our initiatives have provided critical support to businesses in various industries, helping them adapt, scale, and succeed. From providing access to capital to offering strategic guidance, we’ve equipped 3,100 businesses with the tools and resources they need to overcome obstacles and seize opportunities. The results speak for themselves: more jobs created, increased revenue, and businesses that are more resilient in the face of adversity.


Real Stories, Real Impact

The impact of our work is best understood through the stories of the entrepreneurs we've helped. From the family-owned coffee shop that expanded its services to the small clothing brand that grew its online presence, every business we support has a unique journey. These success stories are a testament to the power of community and the collective effort to build a stronger, more sustainable future for small businesses.

Looking Ahead

Our commitment to supporting small and family-owned businesses remains steadfast. We are dedicated to expanding our reach and continuing to provide resources that empower entrepreneurs to grow, succeed, and contribute to the economy. Together, we’re not just supporting businesses—we’re building a legacy of resilience, innovation, and community.


Join us in celebrating the impact we’ve made and the businesses we’ve helped, and stay connected as we continue to support even more small and family-owned businesses in the years to come.
